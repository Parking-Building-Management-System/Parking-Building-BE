Không demo tenant management trong buổi demo này.

6. Demo notes

Do not use a random fingerprint for DEV or staff fallback.
Use seed-dev-vincom-device for DEV.
Use seed-vincom-staff-device for staff fallback.
If the fingerprint changes, backend creates a PENDING device and returns DEVICE_NOT_TRUST.
